Quick hummus recipe

This recipe makes quick, tasty hummus, with no messing.
It has been adapted from a number of different recipes that I have read over the years.

Ingredients

  1. 1 can (400g) of chick peas (garbanzo beans)of tahini
  2. 6 sundried tomatoes
  3. Half a red pepper
  4. A pinch of cayenne pepper
  5. 1 clove of garlic
  6. A dash of olive oil
    7.

Instructions

  • skin from the garlic, and chop coarsely
  • all the seeds and stalk from the pepper, and chop coarsely
  • all the ingredients into a food processor
  • all the ingredients into a paste.
  • you want a coarse "chunky" hummus, process it for a short time
  • you want a smooth hummus, process it for a longer time


A different flavour, you could try blending in a small measure of lemon and coriander, chili pepper, lime and chipotle, harissa and mint, or spinach and feta cheese. Experiment and see what works for you.

Storage

  • Refrigerate the finished hummus in a sealed container.
  • You should be able to use it for about a week after you've made it.
  • If it starts to become fizzy, you should definitely discard it.
  • Hummus is suitable for freezing; you should thaw it and use it within a couple of months.
